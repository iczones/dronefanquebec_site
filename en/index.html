Who is our festival for?

Professional and amateur drone pilots
Curious individuals interested in the subject of drones
Farmers who want to see how drones can help them
Emergency services (firefighters, police) that use or wish to use drones
Landowners who want to have a property mapped
Privacy-conscious individuals who fear being filmed by a drone
Inspectors of roof, high infrastructure, or hard-to-reach location
Novices who do not know the regulation for drones under 250g
Future buyers considering purchasing their first drone
Students in basic, advanced, or specialized pilot training
FPV racing enthusiasts passionate about speed
Content creators who want to improve their footage
Municipal representatives who want to regulate drone use
Young people looking for an exciting career
Airplane pilots and airport managers concerned about accidents

How to get to the site?

Location

Behind CÉGEP Beauce Appalaches
1055 116th Street, Saint-Georges

Via Highway 73

When you reach the end of the highway, turn right to take Route 204 towards the city center. Continue until the second traffic light and turn left onto 120th Street. Go down to 19th Avenue and turn right. Drive to 118th Street. At this point, signs for parking will be visible.

Via Lacroix Boulevard

Go to 118th Street and head up to 19th Avenue. At this point, signs for parking will be visible.

From the west side of the city

Take the bridge and continue straight on 118th Street to 19th Avenue. At this point, signs for parking will be visible.

Important Notes

  • No animals will be allowed on the site,
  • No food, alcohol, or beverages except water will be accepted at the site entrance,
  • Children 12 and under must be accompanied by an adult to access the site,
  • Food truck on site.
